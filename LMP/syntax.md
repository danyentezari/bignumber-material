### Syntax in Linear Algebra

##### Systems of Linear Equations

$2x+y+z=12$
$6x+5y-3z=6$
$4x-y+3z=5$


$\rightarrow$

$\begin{bmatrix}
2 & 1 & 1 & 12 \\
6 & 5 & -3 & 6 \\
4 & -1 & 3 & 5
\end{bmatrix}$

<br/>


##### Matrix Notation
$ \underline{\underline{M}} = \mathbf{M} = \begin{bmatrix}
2 & 1 & 1 & 12 \\
6 & 5 & -3 & 6 \\
4 & -1 & 3 & 5
\end{bmatrix}$

<br/>

##### Vector Notation
$  \vec{v} = \mathbf{v} = [2 \quad 0 \quad 1 \quad 3] $

$\vec{v}^T = \begin{bmatrix}2 \\ 0 \\1 \\3\end{bmatrix}$ 

<br/>

##### Magnitude Notation
$ || v || =  \sqrt{2^2 + 0^2 + 1^2 + 3^2}$
