A vector space is the set of vectors to which the following axioms apply.

(1) Vector addition

u+v∈V\mathbf{u} + \mathbf{v} \in Vu+v∈V

c∈R,v∈Vc \in \mathbb{R},\mathbf{v} \in Vc∈R,v∈V
