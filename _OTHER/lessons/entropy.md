Entropy Function

$$H(X) = - \sum_{i=1}^{n} 
 p(x_i) \times \log_2 p(x_i)$$

where
- $X$ random variable
- $x_i$ some outcome in $X$
- $\log_2 p(x_i)$ information content
- $\sum_{i=1}^{n} p(x_i) \log_2 p(x_i)$ expected information content
