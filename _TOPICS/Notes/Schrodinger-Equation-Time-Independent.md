# Schrodinger Equation Time-Independent

<i>

**definition** (*Schrodinger Equation  -  Time-Independent*) An eigenvalue equation used to determine the stationary states and quantized energy levels of a quantum system when the potential energy is independent of time. It determines the spatial part of the wave function $\psi$ and the allowed energy values $E$ that the system can possess. The generalized form is:

- $\hat{H}\psi = E\psi$

where

- $\hat{H}$ is the Hamiltonian operator representing the total energy of the system
- $\psi$ (lower-case psi) is the spatial wave function (eigenfunction)
- $E$ is the energy of the state (eigenvalue).

</i>
