# Spin

**Electron spin** — Intrinsic angular momentum of an electron, characterized by quantum number 1/2 and represented mathematically by spinors.
*Ref:* Arfken, Weber & Harris, *Mathematical Methods for Physicists*, 7th ed. (2013).

**magnetic moment** — Vector quantity representing the strength and orientation of a magnetic dipole, such as a current loop or spin.
*Ref:* Arfken, Weber & Harris, *Mathematical Methods for Physicists*, 7th ed. (2013); Griffiths, *Introduction to Electrodynamics* (2024).

**spin-orbit coupling** — Interaction between a particle's intrinsic spin and its orbital angular momentum, often expressed as an L·S term.
*Ref:* Arfken, Weber & Harris, *Mathematical Methods for Physicists*, 7th ed. (2013) [citing Condon & Shortley, *Theory of Atomic Spectra*]; "illinois-covariant-contravariant.pdf".
