# Bra and Ket

<i>

**definition [d]** (**Bra and Ket**) A *ket* $|\psi\rangle$ is an element of a complex Hilbert space $\mathcal{H}$; a *bra* $\langle\phi|$ is an element of its dual space $\mathcal{H}^{*}$ acting on kets:

- $\langle\phi| : \mathcal{H} \rightarrow \mathbb{C}, \quad |\psi\rangle \mapsto \langle\phi|\psi\rangle$ .
- $\langle\phi| = |\phi\rangle^{\dagger}$ .

where

- $\mathcal{H}$ is a complex Hilbert space, $\mathcal{H}^{*}$ its dual.
- $\langle\phi|\psi\rangle \in \mathbb{C}$ is the inner product.
- $\dagger$ is the Hermitian conjugate.

</i>
