3 Fourier Transform

Used to transform a signal from its time domain signal to its frequency domain signal.

FFT Time-Frequency View

\[F(x) = \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{2 \pi}} \displaystyle \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} f(u) e^{ixu} \: du\]

Where \(e^{ix} = \cos(x) + i\sin(x)\)
